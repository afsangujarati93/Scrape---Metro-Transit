Service Adjustments

Effective November 27, 2017, as part of the Moving Forward Together Plan, Halifax Transit will make changes to routes 6, 9, 19, 20, 22, and 370.
• Route 6 – Service Discontinued
• Route 9 – Becomes the new Route 29
• Route 19 & 20 – Combined into the new Route 9
• Route 22 – No longer serving Exhibition Park
• Route 370 – No longer serving Micmac Terminal

Also, Halifax Transit will discontinue its shuttle service that has been in operation during the Macdonald Bridge Redecking Project and will return to regular routings.

For a summary of all November service adjustments, please see the November service adjustments quick reference guide. For more information on the upcoming service changes, please see our service adjustments page.
